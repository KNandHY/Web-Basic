ABOUT THE GAME

Dauntless is a massively multiplayer action RPG where you team up with other Slayers to hunt down powerful creatures called Behemoths. Explore the furthest reaches of the Shattered Isles as you quest, craft, and fight to take down increasingly dangerous foes, all while forging your legend as a Slayer of Ramsgate. More
